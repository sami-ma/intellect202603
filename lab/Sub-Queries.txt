Sub queries Example



A) List ManagerName and count of repotees ti this manager


B) List the ManagersName who is having max no.of emps working under him. 


C) List the highest paid emp working under JONES.


D) List the highest paid emp from CHICAGO


E) List the details of most recently hired emp of dept 30


F) List the emp's whose salary  is same as any of other employees. 


G) List the emps whose salary is same as Scott


H) List all employees reporting to Blake


I) List all employees whose salary is more than company average salary


J) List the details of all Managers





Answers:

A) select m.ename,count(*) from emp w,emp m
 where w.mgr = m.empno
 group by m.ename


B)select m.ename,count(*) from emp w,emp m
 where w.mgr = m.empno
 group by m.ename
 having count(*) = (select max(count(*)) from emp group by mgr); 

C) select * from emp where sal in
 (select max(sal) from emp where mgr in
 (select empno from emp where ename = 'JONES')); 


D) select * from emp where sal in 
(select max(sal) from emp where deptno = 
(select deptno from dept where loc='CHICAGO'))


E) select * from emp e1 where e1.deptno=30 and e1.hiredate =
(select max(e3.hiredate) from emp e3 where e3.deptno=30)

F) select * from emp e where e.sal in 
(select e2.sal from emp e2 where e.empno<>e2.empno)

G) select * from emp e1 where e1.sal = 
(select e2.sal from emp e2 where e2.ename='SCOTT')

H) select * from emp where mgr = 
(select empno from emp where ename='BLAKE')

I) select * from emp where sal > 
(select avg(sal) from emp)

J) select * from emp where empno in 
(select mgr from emp)
